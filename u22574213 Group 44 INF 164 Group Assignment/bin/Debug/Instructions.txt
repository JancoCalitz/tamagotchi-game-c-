Rules for Tamagouchi
1. Choose a pet
2. Keep pet fed
3. Pet needs to sleep.
4. Play game and win toys to keep pet happy.
5. Buy and refund books and movies in the pet's collection.
6. If pet is left unsupervised, it will DIE.

Rules for Arcade Game
1. Use space bar to flap up and down
2. Do not fly into green pipes else game will end
3. 1 point for every pipe you pass.
